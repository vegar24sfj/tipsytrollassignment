About us

Welcome to Tipsy Troll Tavern, your friendly neighborhood hideaway where good times, hearty food, and a variety of craft drinks await! Nestled in the heart of the town, our tavern is inspired by a love for great beer, savory comfort food, and a laid-back, welcoming atmosphere. At Tipsy Troll, we believe in the magic of community and the joy of sharing a pint with friends. Whether you're here for one of our carefully brewed house ales, a glass of local wine, or a perfectly crafted cocktail, there's always something to satisfy every taste. Our beer selection features a mix of classic brews, seasonal flavors, and experimental concoctions. We're proud to work with local breweries to bring the best of the region right to your glass. But we’re not just about drinks! Our menu is packed with mouth-watering options, from classic pub fare to creative twists on comfort food. Enjoy our famous hand-cut fries, juicy burgers, artisan pizzas, or dive into something unique, like our signature troll wings or hearty stews. We take pride in serving fresh, high-quality dishes that pair perfectly with every sip. Whether you’re looking to unwind after a long day, grab a bite with friends, or enjoy a fun night out, Tipsy Troll Tavern is the place to be. With live music, great company, and a little bit of whimsy, every visit is an adventure. Come join us at the Tipsy Troll Tavern—where every moment is a celebration and every drink tells a story. Cheers!
